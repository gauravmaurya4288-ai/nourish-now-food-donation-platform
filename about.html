Who We Are

Nourish Now is a food donation platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. We connect restaurants, hotels, caterers, households, NGOs, and volunteers through one simple platform.

Our mission is to ensure that every surplus meal reaches someone who truly needs it instead of ending up in landfills.

Mission

Reduce food waste by connecting donors with NGOs quickly and safely.

Vision

A world where no edible food is wasted and no person sleeps hungry.

Values

Compassion, Transparency, Sustainability and Community.
