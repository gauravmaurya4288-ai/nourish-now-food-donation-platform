Our Mission

Together, we can eliminate food waste and ensure that every meal reaches someone who needs it.

Our Purpose

Nourish Now was founded with one simple belief: no edible food should ever go to waste while people suffer from hunger.

We connect food donors with NGOs and volunteers, creating a seamless bridge between surplus food and those who need it.
